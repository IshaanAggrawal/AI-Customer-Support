
==============================================================================
SECTION 4: TECHNICAL FAQ
==============================================================================

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen. You will receive an email with a reset link 
valid for 1 hour.

Q: Why is my device blinking red?
A: A blinking red light indicates a network connectivity issue. Please hold the reset button 
on the back of the device for 10 seconds to reboot the network module.

Q: Can I share my subscription with others?
A: The "Personal" plan is for a single user only. The "Family" plan allows up to 5 unique 
user accounts under one billing ID. Sharing credentials on a Personal plan may lead to 
account suspension.

Q: How do I contact support?
A: You can email support@techgizmo.com. Our typical response time is 24 hours.
